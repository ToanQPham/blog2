Apparently this fact isn't mentioned anywhere on the Internet. At least not in a straight-forward manner, I had to discover it by inspecting the corresponding articles on Wikipedia.

The code to produce a direct limit and an inverse limit is \varinjlim respectively \varprojlim. This is the result: $$\varinjlim, \varprojlim$$

Let this fact be known.
