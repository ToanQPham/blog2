Last week I gave a talk on the history of TeX and LaTeX (more on this later) and I talked about WEB, the programming language used by Knuth in creating TeX. It follows a paradigm called literate programming and in my opinion it never really took a hold. But if you look at the document source of Backbone you could believe it sure has its benefits. Their entire documentation is beautiful by the way, if only the myriads of underdocumented C++ libraries there are out there had such nice documentation...
