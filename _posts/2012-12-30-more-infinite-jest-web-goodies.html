My love for David Foster Wallace's Infinite Jest has been expressed before, where I linked to the wonderful tumblr Poor Yorick Entertainment. Now there is something new: an involved art project by William Beutler based on Infinite Jest. There is:

  • Infinite Atlas which maps all locations used in the book on Google Maps;
  • Infinite Boston which contains a really thorough discussion of locations in Infinite Jest, including photographs!
  • Infinite Shop where you can buy Infinite Jest related stuff if you feel like supporting the project.

With all this interesting stuff I feel a reread coming up.
