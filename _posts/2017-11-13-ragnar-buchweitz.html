--- layout: post title: Ragnar-Olaf Buchweitz (1952–2017) type: post categories: - mathematics author: Pieter Belmans ---

It is with great sadness that I learned of Ragnar's passing. I remember him as a very kind person and an amazing story-teller, who was extremely broad as a mathematician, and whose talks set the perfect example for me of what a good talk is supposed to be.

See also the announcement at commalg.org.
