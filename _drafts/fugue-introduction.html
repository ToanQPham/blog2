This year I am both finishing my PhD, and finishing my music theory class. I've been studying harmony, counterpoint and composition for a while now, and it's coming together now in writing a project about the fugue versus the classical guitar.

There are several things I intend to write about:

  • give an introduction to the fugue as a form, using examples from the literature for guitar;
  • compare the literature for guitar (including transcriptions from cello and violin) to the literature for piano and organ;
  • analyse various interesting examples, showcasing the specifics of fugues written for guitar.

This weblog used to be called On music, computing and math, and because of this I will be translating some of the more interesting parts whilst I'm writing the actual project in Dutch.

You can find the entire series under the tag 'fugue'.
