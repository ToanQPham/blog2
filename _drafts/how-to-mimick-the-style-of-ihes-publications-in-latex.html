Publications by the IHES are well-known for their specific fonts, at least if you care about this stuff. If not, they are probably known for other reasons, it being one of the most prestigious publishers in the world. For some examples, there are scanned versions of old publications available here. Trying to recreate this style isn't easy, because there are no real ready-to-go packages available for this. But we can try to get close!

The font that is used is proprietary and specifically created to recreate the style and feel of the old publications (TeX wasn't available back then, bien sûr), according to the article Une police mathématique pour la Société Mathématique de France: le SMF Baskerville.
